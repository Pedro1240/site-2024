    About Me

    Hi, I'm Pedro, a 17-year-old IT student with a passion for web design. Throughout my school years, I've tackled various projects, from basic websites to personal blogs. I'm driven by a commitment to excellence, ensuring that each project is both functional and visually appealing. I thrive on challenges and stay updated with the latest design trends. I'm not just a student; I'm a passionate creator, eager to make a meaningful impact through my work in the dynamic field of web design.

    Experience

    I specialize in web designing with proficiency in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, along with a solid grasp of Python and database management. Despite my lack of professional work experience, I have successfully completed projects encompassing basic websites and personal blogs during my school years. My focus on hands-on experience has allowed me to develop practical skills in coding and design, preparing me for future opportunities in the field. I am eager to leverage my knowledge and enthusiasm to contribute to exciting projects and further enhance my capabilities.
